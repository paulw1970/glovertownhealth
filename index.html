The Situation

Why Glovertown Needs a Family Doctor

Access to healthcare is a fundamental need. Right now, residents of Glovertown and the surrounding area face real hardship getting the basic medical care they deserve.

🚗

Long Drives for Basic Care

Without a local physician, residents must travel over an hour to Gander or Clarenville for routine appointments — a serious hardship for seniors and those without reliable transportation.

👴

An Aging Population

A significant portion of Glovertown's residents are seniors who rely on regular medical check-ups, prescription management, and chronic disease care. Distance puts their health at risk.

👨‍👩‍👧

Families & Young Children

Young families need dependable access to pediatric care, immunizations, and urgent appointments. The absence of a local doctor makes raising a family here far more challenging.

🏥

Emergency Room Overload

Without a local GP, residents default to emergency rooms for non-emergency issues, straining regional hospital resources and resulting in longer waits for everyone.

💼

Economic & Growth Impact

Healthcare access is a key factor in where people choose to live and work. Attracting new residents and businesses to Glovertown depends on having essential services in place.

🤝

Community Strength

Glovertown has always looked after its own. By rallying together and showing there is a strong, committed patient base, we can make a compelling case to recruit a physician.

Life in Glovertown

Why You'd Love Living Here

Glovertown isn't just a place to practise medicine — it's a place to build a life. Nestled on the northeast arm of Bonavista Bay, our town offers something rare: a genuine, tight-knit community surrounded by breathtaking natural beauty.

🌊

Stunning Natural Setting

Situated on Bonavista Bay, Glovertown is a gateway to Terra Nova National Park — one of Canada's most scenic parks, right on your doorstep.

🏡

Affordable Cost of Living

Housing is dramatically more affordable than urban centres. A comfortable home with a yard here costs a fraction of what you'd pay in Halifax, Toronto, or St. John's.

🤲

Warm, Welcoming People

Newfoundlanders are world-renowned for their hospitality. In Glovertown, you won't be a stranger for long — you'll be a neighbour, and then a friend.

🎣

Outdoor Recreation

Fishing, kayaking, hiking, snowmobiling, moose hunting — if you love the outdoors, Glovertown delivers world-class recreation right outside your front door.

🏫

Good Schools & Safe Streets

Glovertown is a safe, family-friendly community with local schools and a pace of life that lets kids be kids. It's a wonderful place to raise a family.

💚

Make a Real Difference

As a physician in Glovertown, you won't be one of hundreds — you'll be the doctor. You'll know your patients by name and make a profound, lasting impact on your community.

We're not asking for a lot. We're asking for what every community deserves — a doctor we can call our own.
— The People of Glovertown, Newfoundland
